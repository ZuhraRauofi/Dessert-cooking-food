Chocolate chip cookie

Ingredients

  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 spoon baking powder

Recipe

  1. preheat the oven to 250 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. mix the flour,sugar and baking powder.
  3. add the egg and milk to the mixture.
  4. add the melted chocolate to the mixture.
  5. add the mixture in dish and cook it for 30 mins.
